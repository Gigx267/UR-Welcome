About UR Welcome

Lived in seven St. Louis neighborhoods before I started high school. A a child, I hardly noted any difference in the quality of life and resources between these places, I was loved, protected, and nurtured in each one. All these neighborhoods- all My neighborhoods, were home. UR Welcome is a dedication to the beauty in my upbringing. It is acknowledgement of the struggle and the deep resilience of a city made of communities that won't give up. UR Welcome is an invitation to use strengths of our diverse communities to address our city's delicate needs. I hope this organization can begin to bridge the social and physical gaps that keep St. Louisians apart. UR Welcome is my personal gift to a city that kept me safe, happy, and entertained. May this be a contribution towards a Greater more unified St. Louis where strangers become neighbors, and every community feels like home.

OUR MISSION
To utilize the strengths of our city as solutions to our challenges, transforming St. Louis into a unified community renowned for its kindness, care, and support. Through opportunities to build meaningful connections and serve our community, UR Welcome aims to give St. Louis-- inclusive of all its neighborhoods, all its communities, and all its residents--the support needed to reach greatest potential.

OUR VISION
Where a culture of service, connection, and support ensure strangers become neighbors and every STL community feels like home. At UR Welcome, everyone belongs to one community. We embrace a spirit of generosity and support. We are all neighbors, ready to serve and eager to connect

SOCIAL TRANSFORMATION
UR Welcome was created to inspire lasting social change in St. Louis by addressing social and physical barriers. We envision a future where every resident can thrive in a supportive and inclusive environment. By aligning interests and resources, we can generate positive social impact.

A UNIFIED & COHESIVE ST. LOUIS

We bridge gaps between St. Louis neighborhoods and residents, enhancing social capital. UR Welcome's projects promote unity, strengthen community bonds, and cultivate a sense of care and cohesion among residents from diverse backgrounds.
